# Security Policy

## Reporting a vulnerability

Please report security issues **privately** — do not open a public GitHub issue.

Email **security@aaagents.de** with:

- A clear description of the issue and the affected component
- Steps to reproduce, ideally with a minimal proof-of-concept
- Your assessment of impact and any suggested mitigation
- Whether you would like to be credited in the public disclosure

We aim to acknowledge reports within **5 business days** and to issue a fix
or coordinated public disclosure within **90 days** of acknowledgement.
For embargoed issues, we will coordinate a release window with you.

PGP key for encrypted reports: available on request.

## Scope

This policy covers the **AAAgents Community Edition** open-source
components in this repository. The hosted enterprise platform at
`aaagents.de` is out of scope here — please email the same address and
mark the subject `[platform]`.

## Out of scope

- Reports generated solely by automated scanners with no demonstrated
  impact
- Issues in third-party dependencies — please report those upstream
  first; we will track and apply patches
- Social-engineering, physical-access, or denial-of-service findings
  against test deployments

## Safe-harbor

Good-faith security research conducted under this policy will not result
in legal action from us. We expect researchers to avoid privacy
violations, service disruption, and data destruction during testing.
